# Workpilot Community

Building a practical community around AI-powered software products.

---

## Purpose

Workpilot Community exists to support people around the Workpilot ecosystem.

Products grow stronger when users, developers, partners, and contributors can learn, share, and collaborate.

---

## Mission

Create a practical and welcoming community for people interested in Workpilot products, AI business automation, and useful software for real businesses.

---

## Who This Community Is For

Workpilot Community is for:

- Business owners
- Developers
- Product builders
- AI enthusiasts
- Partners
- Contributors
- Customers and users

---

## What Workpilot Community Covers

### Announcements

Updates about Workpilot products and initiatives.

Examples:

- Product updates
- New releases
- Roadmap updates
- Community notices

---

### Discussions

A place for practical conversations.

Topics include:

- AI for business
- Customer engagement
- Business automation
- Product ideas
- Developer collaboration

---

### Support

Helping users understand and use Workpilot products.

Support areas may include:

- Chioma
- MerchantPilot
- Future Workpilot products
- Documentation guidance
- Integration questions

---

### Tutorials

Learning resources for users and developers.

Examples:

- Product walkthroughs
- Setup guides
- API usage guides
- Business automation examples

---

### Showcase

Highlighting useful work from the Workpilot ecosystem.

Examples:

- Product demos
- Community projects
- Case studies
- Practical AI use cases
